About Us

The Sudanese Society of Breast Imaging & Women’s Health Network (SSBIWHN) is a professional, non‑profit community of radiologists, surgeons, oncologists, pathologists, technologists and trainees dedicated to best practice in breast imaging and multidisciplinary care.

Our mission is to strengthen education, research, and equitable access to high‑quality breast care.

Our Objectives

Education

Deliver high‑quality training and continuous professional development in breast imaging.

Research

Enable collaborative research and audit projects addressing regional needs.

Guidelines

Develop practical guidance tailored to low‑ and middle‑income settings.

MDT Culture

Promote teamwork across radiology, surgery, oncology and pathology.

Activities

  • Annual symposia & focused workshops
  • Monthly webinars and virtual case conferences
  • Teaching files and e‑learning resources
  • Community awareness and outreach
